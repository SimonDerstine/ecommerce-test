What are microgreens?

Microgreens are young, tender greens harvested just after the first leaves appear. They are packed with flavor and nutrients, and can be grown in a small tray on a sunny windowsill using only seeds, soil, and water.

Simon's Garden focuses on simple, beginner-friendly seed pouches so you can enjoy fresh greens without special equipment or a backyard.
